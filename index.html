Edward Stallknecht Rice

I am a senior research scientist in the lab of Prof. Wes Warren at the University of Missouri. I received a Ph.D. in Bioinformatics in 2018 from University of California, Santa Cruz. I use bioinformatics to study evolutionary, molecular, and organismal biology.
